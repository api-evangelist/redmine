<p>Redmine <strong><a class="version" href="https://www.redmine.org/versions/216">7.0.0</a></strong> has been released and it is available for download from <a class="wiki-page" href="https://www.redmine.org/projects/redmine/wiki/Download">Download</a>. This release ships 122 issues, with "Webhooks triggers on evens" as major feature, Rails 8 migration, RTL improved support, header/navigation redesign, and continued UI modernization started in 6.0 and 6.1.</p> <p><strong>This release also marks 20 years since Redmine's first release in 2006</strong> (<a class="changeset" title="Initial setup" href="https://www.redmine.org/projects/redmine/repository/svn/revisions/1">r1</a> committed by <a class="user active" href="https://www.redmine.org/users/1">Jean-Philippe Lang</a> on 28.06.2006). Two decades later, the project is still actively maintained and improved thanks to a community of contributors, users, and companies who keep investing their time and effort into it.
