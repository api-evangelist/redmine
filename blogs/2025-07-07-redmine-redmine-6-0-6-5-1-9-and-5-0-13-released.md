<p>Maintenance releases <a class="version" href="https://www.redmine.org/versions/211">6.0.6</a>, <a class="version" href="https://www.redmine.org/versions/210">5.1.9</a> and <a class="version" href="https://www.redmine.org/versions/212">5.0.13</a> are now available to <a class="wiki-page" href="https://www.redmine.org/projects/redmine/wiki/Download">Download</a>, bringing a total of 32 bug fixes (<a class="wiki-page" href="https://www.redmine.org/projects/redmine/wiki/Changelog">Changelog</a>).</p> <p>All three releases contain a new version of <code>net-imap</code> gem to address <a href="https://www.ruby-lang.org/en/news/2025/04/28/dos-net-imap-cve-2025-43857" class="external">CVE-2025-43857</a> (#42662) and also some important fixes to improve the compatibility of Redmine with Rack >= 3.1.14 (<a class="issue tracker-1 status-5 priority-4 priority-default closed" title="Defect: &quot;Page not found&quot; error when saving workflows with many statuses on Rack &gt;= 3.1.14 (Closed)" href="https://www.redmine.org/issues/42875">#42875</a>, <a class="issue tracker-1 status-5 priority-4 priority-default closed" title="Defect: Mail handler fails to create issues from emails over 4MB on Rack &gt;= 3.1.14 (Closed)" href="https://www.redmine.org/issues/42962">#42962</a>). Additionally, the patch for <a class="issue tracker-3 status-5 priority-4 priority-default closed" title="Patch: Limit available locales to those defined by Redmine itself (Closed)" href="https://www.redmine.org/issues/38529">#38529</a>, initially released in version <a class="version" href="https://www.redmine.org/versions/176">5.1.0</a>, is now properly fixed and the locales are limited to those defined by Redmine itself.</p> <p>Thanks everyone for their contributions.</p>
