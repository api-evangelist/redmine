<p>At <a href="https://www.redmine.org/" class="external">www.redmine.org</a>, we have made two-factor authentication (2FA) mandatory for all users to help prevent spam and strengthen security.</p> <p>If you have not enabled 2FA on <a class="external" href="http://www.redmine.org">www.redmine.org</a>, you will be asked to set it up the next time you sign in. Please follow the on-screen instructions and use a TOTP-compatible authentication app such as Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, Authy, or Duo Mobile.</p> <p><strong>Background</strong></p> <p>In recent years, we have seen an increase in spam and test submissions on Redmine.org. To address this problem and to better protect user accounts, we have made 2FA mandatory.</p> <p>Two-factor authentication also greatly reduces the risk of account takeovers and improves the overall security of the site.</p> <p>While the registration process for new users will take a bit more effort, it may help reduce spam and test submissions to some extent.</p> <p>We apologize for the extra steps required to sign in and appreciate your cooperation.</p>
